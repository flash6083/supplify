About

SUPPLIFY aims to eradicate supply chain disruptions that are often caused by various reasons such as Fluctuations in transport, Weather changes, Pandemics, Middle-man discrepancy, etc. Supplify provides a platform where buyers or sellers can interact with each other independently in an Online Marketplace. It's underlying machine learning algorithms predict the market and weather, thus providing a complete analytical report every user's personalised dashboard.
